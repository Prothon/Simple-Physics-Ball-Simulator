🕹️ How to Play
Click + Drag → Place & throw the ball
Right Click or Two-finger tap → Drop a gravity well 🪐
X → Clear all gravity wells
R → Reset everything
F → Toggle force arrows
T → Toggle trail
H → Toggle formulas
G → Toggle gravity well detail
Space → Pause / unpause
📱 On mobile: drag to throw, two-finger tap for wells, three-finger tap to pause, ⚙️ for controls
